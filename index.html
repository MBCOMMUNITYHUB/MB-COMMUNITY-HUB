About Our Community Hub

Welcome to the Myrtle Beach Community Hub — a welcoming space dedicated to bringing neighbors together through social events, skill-sharing workshops, mutual aid initiatives, and meaningful community action.

Our mission is simple: to create a stronger, more connected Myrtle Beach where everyone feels seen, supported, and empowered. Whether you're new to town, a lifelong local, a student, a small business owner, or simply someone looking to meet like-minded people — you belong here.

We host gatherings that inspire creativity, promote wellness, encourage civic engagement, and uplift local voices. Together, we can build a more compassionate and resilient community.

Community Events & Workshops

From beach clean-ups and creative workshops to community potlucks and discussion circles, our calendar is full of opportunities to connect and contribute.

We encourage members to host their own events as well — if you have an idea that brings people together, we would love to help you make it happen.
